Moreton Jazz

This sleepy Cotswolds village is the home of a relatively new jazz club. Since April 2016, each month we have welcomed a new line-up of extraordinary jazz musicians and singers for a night of top-notch live music.

Dunchurch Jazz Club

Now in it’s third year, Dunchurch Jazz Club is a monthly jazz evening bringing the best in UK jazz to Warwickshire. With Ben in residence at the village hall each jazz night, every month sees a new, exciting line-up that will appeal both to jazz and live music lovers alike.

Market Bosworth Jazz Club

This monthly jazz evening at Market Bosworth Parish Hall is now in its second year. Having welcomed an array of fabulous musicians throughout 2016, 2017 promises to welcome even more of the UK’s jazz talent to Leicestershire.

Mickleover Golf Club Jazz Sessions

Located just outside Derby city centre, the beautiful grounds of Mickleover Golf Club play host once a month to a night of exciting jazz. This intimate setting has seen some phenomenal musicians come to Derby and 2017 will be no exception!

Jazz on Tap @ The National Brewery Centre

Two of life’s joys are jazz and fine ale and 'Jazz on Tap' combines both of these! In the brilliant setting of Burton’s National Brewery Centre you’ll find a monthly jazz night catering for live music lovers in and around Burton on Trent.
